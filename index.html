ABOUT US

At JAY Home Healthcare Services, we're committed to delivering personalized, high-quality medical care in the comfort of your own home. Our comprehensive services cater to individuals requiring assistance with healthcare management due to aging, illness, recovery, or rehabilitation. .

OUR SERVICES

  • Comprehensive in-home care for individuals with chronic conditions, post-operative recovery needs, or rehabilitation requirements

  • Personalized care plans developed in collaboration with healthcare professionals and tailored to each client's unique needs.

  • Skilled nursing services including wound care, patient education, intravenous therapy, and injections.

  • Rehabilitation services such as physical therapy, occupational therapy, and speech-language pathology

  • Assistance with activities of daily living (ADLs), including bathing, dressing, and meal preparation.

  • Provision of medical social services to support clients and their families during challenging times.
